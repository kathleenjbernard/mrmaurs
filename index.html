About Mr. Maurs

Mr. Maurs was created to provide barbecue enthusists from all walks of life with a high-quality barbecue sauce made from more than just sugar and filler ingredients - at an affordable price.

Spencer Lindsay, founder of Mr. Maurs, has always had a knack for flavor. In early 2013, Spencer began working as a security guard at Amazon's Corporate Headquarters in Seattle, Washington. After bringing his homemade barbecue chicken wings to a company potluck and experiencing an overwhelmingly positive response, Spencer saw an opportunity. With the support of family and natural entrepreneurial instincts, Mr. Maurs was created.

Since then, Spencer has been featured on the cover of The Seattle Times, selected to ring the NASDAQ Bell in New York City with Amazon CEO Jeff Bezos and participated in a roundtable with SBA Administrator, Linda McMahon.
Spencer ultimately plans on opening a retail storefront as well as a Manufacturing and Distribution Facility. He hopes to be a great example for his daughter - by showing her that she can do anything she sets her mind to.
